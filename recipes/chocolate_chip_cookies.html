Chocolate Chip Cookies

The most popular of them all!

Imagine a crunchy buttery outside with a soft chewy inside. Be careful how many cookies you make. You may have a hard time putting these down!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (room temperature)
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven at 375 F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, comine butter, shortening, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla. Beat until creamy.
  4. Beat in eggs. Gradually add in flour mixture.
  5. WIth a spatula, fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop dough by rounded tbsp-fuls onto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until golden brown.
