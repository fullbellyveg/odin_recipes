Zhoug Sauce

A flavorlful Middle Eastern green chili sauce

This version is simple and easy without compromising on flavor. Lather it over grilling meats, spoon it into falafels and wraps, liven up soups and stews or make Zhoug yogurt!

Ingredients

  • 1-3 jalapenos, sliced
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 1 bunch cilantro, small stems ok
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamon
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients except oil into food processor and pulse until uniformly chopped.
  2. Add oil and lemon juice, process into a corse paste.
  3. Add salt and chili flakes to taste.
  4. Store in a sealed jar in the fridge for up to a week.
