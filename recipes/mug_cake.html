Chocolate Mug Cake

A quick and easy dessert that fits in a mug

The best part about this recipe is it makes a single serving. You likely already have most of these ingredients in your kitchen. Make sure to top it off with fruit, ice cream, or whipped cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup milk (or 3 tbsp milk + 1 tbsp creamer)

Directions

  1. In a 9-10 oz mug, melt butter (about 30 seconds in microwave).
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the mug.
  3. Microwave on highest setting for 90 seconds. Make sure mug is placed off center.
  4. Let sit for 2-3 minutes. Add favorite toppings if desired.
