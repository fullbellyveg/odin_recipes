Beef and Broccoli

A simple stir fry dish with a delicious teriyaki sauce

This dish is perfect for a fast and easy meal. Serve over rice or noodles. If you like to add spice, I always recommend 1-2 tsp spicy chili oil be added to the sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank (or stir fry) steak, thinly sliced
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 lb broccoli, chopped and halved florets

For the Sauce

  • 1 tsp fresh ginger grated
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 4-6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil

Directions

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl, stir well to dissolve the sugar, and set aside
  2. Place a large skillet over medium heat and add 1 tbsp oil.
  3. Add broccoli florets and saute 4-5 min, partially covered with lid, stirring or tossing several times until broccoli is crisp tender, then remove.
  4. Increase heat to high heat and add 1 tbsp oil. Add beef and saute just until cooked through.
  5. Add the sauce, reduce heat to medium low and simmer 3-4 min or until sauce has thickened. Add broccoli and stir to combine. Serve.
