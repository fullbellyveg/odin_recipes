Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

An upgrade to the old-fashioned chocolate chip cookie

Enjoy these soft, moist cookies that are a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup canned pumpkin
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 - 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add pumpkin, egg, and vanilla to butter mixture.
  5. Slowly add in flour mixture.
  6. With a spatula, fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Frop 1.5 inch diameter rounds onto baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
